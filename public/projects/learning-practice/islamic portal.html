Facts About the Kaaba

House of Allah

The Kaaba is known as "Baitullah" (House of Allah) and is the holiest site in Islam.

Rebuilt Several Times

The Kaaba has been reconstructed up to 12 times throughout history.

Qibla Direction

Muslims worldwide face the Kaaba during prayers, making it the Qibla.

Black Stone

The Hajar al-Aswad (Black Stone) is believed to have come from Paradise.
